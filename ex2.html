Temperament-Related Titles & Evaluations
BH/VTBegleithundprüfung / Verkehrstest
The BH/VT is the mandatory temperament and traffic-safety test required before any IGP title. The dog must demonstrate stable behavior in public, around crowds, traffic, and other dogs. Sagi passed this evaluation confirming his neutral, stable temperament in all settings.
TSB: PTriebveranlagung, Selbstsicherheit, Belastbarkeit — Pronounced
TSB evaluates three critical traits: drive, self-assurance, and stress capacity. "Pronounced" is the highest possible rating — confirming Sagi's exceptional nerve strength and ability to handle pressure while remaining stable and clear-headed.
KKLKörklasse — Lifetime Breed Survey
A Lifetime Breed Survey evaluates the dog's character, working drives, and temperament alongside structure. Sagi's survey confirmed his excellent temperament — approachable, confident, and reliable under all conditions.
FOFamily Obedience
FO demonstrates the dog's ability to behave reliably and calmly in everyday family and public settings — reflecting Sagi's dual nature as both a high-drive working dog and a trustworthy family companion.

Working Titles
IGP3Internationale Gebrauchshund Prüfung 3
IGP3 is the highest level of working dog sport — testing tracking, obedience, and protection at the most demanding level. The dog must demonstrate strong nose work across long aged tracks, reliable off-leash obedience in distracting environments, and confident protection work including searching, holding, and escorting.
IGP2Internationale Gebrauchshund Prüfung 2
IGP2 tests intermediate-advanced tracking, obedience, and protection skills. It requires longer tracks, more complex obedience patterns, and more demanding protection scenarios than IGP1.
IGP1Internationale Gebrauchshund Prüfung 1
IGP1 is the entry-level working dog title in the international system, testing basic tracking, obedience, and protection. Even this foundation level requires significant training and natural ability.
FOFamily Obedience
FO (Family Obedience) demonstrates the dog's ability to behave reliably and calmly in everyday family and public settings.
BH/VTBegleithundprüfung / Verkehrstest
The BH/VT is the mandatory temperament and traffic-safety test required before any IGP title. The dog must demonstrate stable behavior in public, around crowds, traffic, and other dogs.
ADAusdauerprüfung (Endurance Test)
The AD requires the dog to trot alongside a bicycle for 20 kilometers (12.4 miles) at a steady pace, followed by a simple obedience check.
Breed Survey & Conformation
KKLKörklasse — Lifetime Breed Survey
A Lifetime Breed Survey (KKL) is an extensive evaluation of the dog's structure, temperament, and working ability conducted by a certified breed surveyor. Sagi's lifetime survey was conducted by USCA and carries a TSB rating of "Pronounced" — the highest possible drive and self-assurance assessment.
TSB: PTriebveranlagung, Selbstsicherheit, Belastbarkeit — Pronounced
TSB evaluates three critical traits: drive, self-assurance, and stress capacity. "Pronounced" is the highest possible rating.
Ang*Angekört — Surveyed / Approved for Breeding
The Angekört designation means the dog has passed a breed survey and has been officially approved for breeding. The asterisk (*) indicates recommended for breeding.
VDVorzüglich (Czech Show Rating)
VD ("Very Good") in the Czech conformation system evaluates the dog's physical structure against the breed standard.
G1Good (USCA Show Rating)
The USCA conformation rating evaluates the dog's physical structure in the American branch of the SV system.
